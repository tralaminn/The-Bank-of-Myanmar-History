Featured Timeline

  1. City-States Era: Pre-9th century — early kingdoms and trade hubs (Thaton, Pyu city-states, Dvaravati influences).
  2. Bagan Kingdom: 9th–13th centuries — temple-building and Theravada consolidation.
  3. Taungoo & Konbaung: 16th–19th centuries — reunification, expansion, and royal patronage of the arts.
  4. Colonial Period: 1824–1948 — gradual annexation, social change, and nationalist movements.
  5. Post-Independence: 1948–present — independence, modern state-building, and cultural revival.

About the Project

The Bank of Myanmar History is a collaborative student project meant to collect, curate, and present Myanmar's historical eras in a modern, accessible format. Each archive is crafted with visual design and coded interactions so history can be explored by anyone.

Want to contribute images, primary sources, or help with translations? Reach out — this is a growing vault and we welcome new deposits.
